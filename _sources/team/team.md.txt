# Team

GEOMAPI is developped as a joint project within the GEOMATICS lab of the KU Leuven in Ghent, Belgium. This project has received funding from the VLAIO BAEKELAND programme (grant agreement HBC.2020.2819) together with MEET HET BV, the VLAIO COOCK project (grant agreement HBC.2019.2509),  the VLAIO BAEKELAND programme (grant agreement HBC.2022.0153) together with BAUWENS NV, the FWO Postdoc grant (grant agreement 1251522N) and the Geomatics research group of the Department of Civil Engineering, TC Construction at the KU Leuven in Belgium.
